Step 2: The White Cross

Goal: Build a white cross on the white-center face and align each edge’s side color with its matching side-center. This organizes the cube so the first layer inserts cleanly.

From Daisy → Cross:

  • For each white edge on U, spin U until the side color matches its side-center.
  • Turn that face F2 (a half-turn) to flip the edge down next to the white center.
  • If an edge is flipped in the bottom layer, lift it out (e.g., R U R'), realign on U, then F2.

Checker logic: We verify four things: the white edge sits next to the white center, the white sticker faces the white face, and the edge’s side sticker matches the adjacent side-center color. When all 4/4 are correct, Continue unlocks.

Tip: You can rotate the whole cube freely — the checker reads true 3D geometry, not just what’s “up” on screen.
