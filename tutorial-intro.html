Welcome! What even is this cube?

A quick tour: origin, rise, comps, world records, and how big the cube world actually is today.

Origins

Invented in 1974 by Hungarian architect Ernő Rubik; first sold locally in 1977 and worldwide from 1980. It exploded into pop culture and remains the best-selling puzzle toy ever.

All-time units sold

~500,000,000+
Across official Rubik’s & third-party brands (directional).

Official brand (recent year)

≈5–6M units
Rubik’s brand sell-in varies by year/source.

Competitions (recent year)

~2,500–2,700 events
~100 countries; tens of thousands of competitors.

How big is the cube business?

There’s no single official “lifetime revenue” number. Public market write-ups put the annual cube/puzzle segment roughly in the high hundreds of millions to low billions of USD. Treat these as directional.

Popular speedcube brands beyond Rubik’s include GAN, MoYu, and QiYi/MoFangGe.
